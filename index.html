Basic Information

Let's set up the baseline details for your assessment.

1. Are they demanding upfront charges for things like registration, training courses, processing fees, or dynamic corporate assets?

2. How did the company reach out or coordinate this recruitment pipeline with you?

3. Does the company have a clear online presence (such as a working corporate website or LinkedIn organizational profile)?

4. Was this internship offer handed out without any formal interview, selection test, or conversational validation?

5. Did the entity offer you a structured legal document, signed appointment agreement, or formal letterhead document?

6. Are they pushing for highly confidential data assets (like banking keys, system credentials, or full national ID paperwork) before any official contract execution?

7. Does the offer sound unrealistic (such as exceptional high pay rates for minimal hours, or zero prior technical skill prerequisites)?

8. Are recruiters applying high-pressure timeline strategies, saying slots are disappearing or demanding immediate payment choices?

9. What type of technical email setup or domain footprint did their representative use to communicate with you?

10. Is the projected stipend structure heavily inflated compared to competitive enterprise standard baselines?
